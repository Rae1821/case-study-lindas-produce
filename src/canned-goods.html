Canned Goods, Cheese, & Meats

Come see our large variety of canned goods, cheeses, and meats from brands such as Vidalia Peanut Company, Whistlestop, Tennessee's Best, Amish Wedding, Mayfield Milk, Sara Lee, and many more! *All items may not be available at both locations.*

Local Honey

Did you know consuming local honey may help with seasonal allergies? We carry local honey from Birchwood, Tennessee, Sand-Mountain, Alabama, and Del Rio, Tennessee.

Meats

Hickory smoked bacon, smoked sausage, bologna, trail rings, and summer sausage are just a few of the meats you'll find here at Linda's.

Butter

Fresh and natural Amish butter in 2 lb. and 10oz. rolls.

Cheese

Fresh and natural Amish made cheese available in half-moons and wedges. Some varieties offered include: Chedder Hoop Cheese, Natural Farmer, Colby, Marble, Ligntening Jack, Pepper Jack, & Buttercheese.

Canned Goods

You will find a variety of Amish Wedding, Tennessee's Best, + Sand Mountain Sorghym Co. products at Linda's.

Popular items include: Chow-Chow, pickles, baby beets, hot and mild pickled okra, pickled eggs, saurkraut, salsa, relishes, dilly corn, sliced jalapenos peaches, pears, & apple BBQ sauce.

Jams + Jellies

We have Amish wedding jams and jellies + a selection of naturally sweetened jams.

Some favorites include: Apple Butter, Seedless Blackberry, Pumpkin Butter, Concord Grape, Red Raspberry Jalapeno, Strawberry Rhubarb, & Fig.

Salad Dressing

You will find a wide variety of salad dressings and marinades that were specially made for Linda's Produce.

Some favorites include: Non-fat Vidalia Onion, Summer Tomato, Creamy Vidalia Onion, Vidalia Onion Creamy Cucumber, Organic Roasted Garlic Vinaigrette, Vidalia Onion Ranch, Lemon Pepercorn, + Vidalia Onion Poppy Seed.

Snacks

Boiled peanuts, lady finger popcorn, pork rinds, roasted peanuts, beef jerky, orange slices, chocolate covered candy (seasonal).

Batters + Mixes

Fried Green Tomato, Chili Starter, Caboose Cobbler, Apple Crisp, Hushpuppy, Onion Rings, Fruit Fritter, Potato Seasonings.
